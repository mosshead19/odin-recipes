Simple Adobo

Ingredients

  • onion
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • 3 cups of water
  • msg
  • 1kg pork

Steps

  1. Mix chopped pork with onion, garlic, peppers, soy sauce, 2 cups of water, and MSG.
  2. Heat the pan and cook the mix until almost dry
  3. Add water as needed to soften the pork
